1. Introduction

MIHAINTELLIGENCE SRL ("we", "us", "our") is a Romanian limited liability company registered in Romania and operating under the laws of the European Union, including the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by MIHAINTELLIGENCE SRL on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, as well as any associated backend services. It describes what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and the rights you have as a data subject.

Scope: This policy covers any app distributed by MIHAINTELLIGENCE SRL. Individual apps may display a supplementary in-app notice if they collect data beyond what is described here.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only the data that is necessary for the features and performance of our applications. The categories below reflect what our apps may collect; each app's App Store / Play Store listing identifies which categories apply to that specific app.

3.1 Account & Registration Data

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

  • Full name or display name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a one-way cryptographic hash — never in plain text)
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)
  • Authentication tokens from third-party sign-in providers (Sign in with Apple, Google)

3.2 Usage & Interaction Data

  • In-app actions and feature usage (e.g., screens visited, buttons tapped)
  • Session duration and frequency of use
  • Search queries entered within the app
  • Content created, edited or deleted by you within the app

3.3 Device & Technical Data

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version (iOS / Android)
  • App version
  • Language and locale settings
  • Time zone
  • Anonymized or pseudonymized device identifier (IDFV on iOS; Android ID on Android)
  • IP address (processed transiently for security; not stored long-term)

3.4 Push Notification Tokens

If you grant permission, we collect a push notification token issued by Apple (APNs) or Google (FCM) to deliver in-app notifications. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device Settings.

3.5 Crash Reports & Diagnostics

We use crash reporting tools to collect stack traces and diagnostic logs when the app crashes or encounters an error. These reports help us identify and fix bugs. They are pseudonymized and do not intentionally include personal content you have created.

3.6 Location Data

We only collect precise or approximate location data if a specific feature of the app requires it, and only after requesting explicit permission. The in-app permission dialog will clearly explain why location access is needed. You may deny or revoke this permission at any time.

3.7 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment card numbers or banking details (handled by App Store / Google Play billing)
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, or files from your device without an explicit feature request and your permission
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for automated profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects

5. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We use a limited number of third-party processors to operate our apps. All processors are contractually bound by a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) and are GDPR-compliant. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

  • Cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS, GCP): Hosts our backend servers and databases within EU or EU-adequate regions where possible.
  • Crash reporting (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics, Sentry): Receives pseudonymized diagnostic data to help us fix bugs.
  • Analytics (optional, consent-gated): Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics to understand feature adoption.
  • Push notification providers (APNs / FCM): Apple and Google deliver push messages; your notification token is shared with the relevant platform provider.
  • Authentication providers (Apple, Google): If you use "Sign in with Apple" or "Sign in with Google," these providers share a token and, at your option, a name and email with us.

5.1 International Transfers

Some processors may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place — such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission or adequacy decisions under GDPR Chapter V.

5.2 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your data if required to do so by applicable law, court order, or government authority, or to protect our legal rights, the safety of users, or the integrity of our services.

6. Data Retention

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject under the GDPR, you have the following rights. You may exercise any of these rights free of charge by contacting us at privacy@mihaintelligence.io. We will respond within 30 days; complex cases may be extended by a further 60 days with notice.

Art. 15

Right of Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, along with information on how and why we process it.

Art. 16

Right to Rectification

Ask us to correct inaccurate personal data or complete incomplete data we hold about you.

Art. 17

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten") where there is no overriding legal basis for continued processing.

Art. 18

Right to Restriction

Ask us to suspend processing of your data in specific circumstances, for example while a rectification request is pending.

Art. 20

Right to Portability

Receive a copy of data you provided to us in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV) to transfer to another service.

Art. 21

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) or to processing for direct marketing purposes at any time.

Art. 7(3)

Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.

Art. 77

Right to Lodge a Complaint

File a complaint with the Romanian supervisory authority (ANSPDCP) or the DPA of your EU member state of residence.

8. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 16 (or the applicable minimum digital consent age in the user's EU member state). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under this age limit.

If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided us with personal data without consent, please contact us at privacy@mihaintelligence.io. We will delete the data promptly upon verification.

Apps specifically designed for children or families will include a separate, reinforced privacy notice compliant with App Store and Play Store family policy requirements.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, accidental loss, destruction, or disclosure, in line with GDPR Article 32.

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 or equivalent
  • Passwords are hashed using a strong one-way algorithm (bcrypt or Argon2)
  • Access to production data is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis
  • We conduct regular security reviews and dependency audits
  • In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours and affected users without undue delay, in line with GDPR Articles 33–34

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  1. Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  2. Display an in-app notice or send an email notification to registered users
  3. Where required by law, request renewed consent before the changes take effect

Continued use of our applications after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes acceptance of the new terms, to the extent permitted by applicable law. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
